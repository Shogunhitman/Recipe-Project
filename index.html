Rock Candy


  • (12.0z) Mason Jar
  • (7-inch) pieces clean kitchen string
  • wooden skewers or pencils
  • 1/2 cups sugar
  • 2 cups water
  • Food Colouring (Optional)

Ingredients

  1. In a large saucepan add sugar and water at medium low heat boil until boils to 300 to 319F
  2. lay it on a cookie tray with parchment paper on
  3. Wait atleast 4-7 hours to harden
  4. Enjoy!
